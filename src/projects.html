Director's Statement

We intend for this show to be a fictional series about finding love AND to simultaneously be a nimble lifestyle ad that actively partners with brands. We will weave products and branded content directly into our native storylines. The main characters are played by real life influencers and they incorporate brands and product placement directly into their plot lines. We focus on great storytelling and character development so when we embed a brand or product into the show, we already have the trust and interest of the viewers.
